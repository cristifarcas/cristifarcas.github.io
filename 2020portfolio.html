Glofox.com was an amazing opportunity to get on board with a fast-paced, innovative start-up offering management software solutions for gyms and yoga studios.

STORY MAPPING AND CUSTOMER JURNEY

I used this essential technique to visualise stories about developed software solutions and its users. The mapping helped me improve my understanding of particular solutions, identify gaps, and define implementation slices.

PROTOTYPING AND RAPID TESTING

To verify assumptions, find blind spots and quickly validate solutions, I used various testing environments such as Invision, Hotjar, usabilityhub.com and direct user behaviour observation.

DESIGNS PAIRED WITH BUSINESS RULES

Working closely with the project owners, I paired the designed assets with the business rules by using the same naming convention (e.g ID-1). This has created an enhanced clarity, streamlined production by reducing back-and-forward discussion.
